Dutch Colonial Era

From the 17th century, the Dutch colonized Indonesia, exploiting its resources and people. The struggle against colonial rule lasted for centuries, shaping a strong sense of unity and identity among Indonesians.

Independence

On August 17, 1945, Indonesia proclaimed independence. Led by Sukarno and Hatta, Indonesians fought bravely to defend their freedom. After years of struggle, Indonesia was finally recognized as an independent nation in 1949.

Modern Indonesia

Today, Indonesia is a vibrant democracy, rich in culture and diversity, and is the largest economy in Southeast Asia. Its journey from ancient kingdom to modern nation is a story of resilience and unity.
